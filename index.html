Conferences are a gift — dozens of speakers, months of prep, all pulled together so the rest of us can learn in a week. Many talks are genuinely excellent.

But lineups have also drifted toward sponsor showcases, and plenty restate common knowledge, demo a product, or dress marketing up as insight. This list tries to surface the talks that would be worth your time.

How quality is scored

Each video is scored by an LLM on five dimensions (0–10): substance, evidence, specificity, insight density, and non-promotion.

The composite (0–100) is a weighted sum — substance ×3, evidence ×2, specificity ×1.5, insight ×2.5, non-promotion ×1 — with a small adjustment for relative like count. Click any score badge for a per-talk breakdown.
